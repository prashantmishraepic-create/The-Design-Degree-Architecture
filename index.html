About Us

Creating Spaces That Inspire

The Design Degree Architecture is a premier architecture and interior design firm based in Pune, specializing in creating innovative and sustainable design solutions. Located in the heart of Erandwane, we bring over years of expertise in transforming spaces.

Our multidisciplinary team of architects, interior designers, and project managers work collaboratively to deliver exceptional results that exceed client expectations. We believe in the power of thoughtful design to enhance quality of life.

FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our services.

What services do you provide?

+
We offer comprehensive architecture and interior design services including residential design, commercial spaces, space planning, renovation, and complete project management from concept to completion.

How long does a project typically take?

+
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 2-4 months, while commercial projects may require 4-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide 3D visualization?

+
Yes, we provide detailed 3D renderings and walkthroughs of your project before execution begins, allowing you to visualize the final outcome.

What areas do you serve?

+
We primarily serve Pune and Mumbai, but also take on select projects across Maharashtra and other major cities in India.

How do you charge for your services?

+
We offer customized pricing based on project scope, size, and requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed quote.
